# 1. PURPOSE & READING CONTRACT

This document exists to be attacked at the architectural level.

It is not a pitch, not a proposal, not an endorsement request and not a validation exercise. It does not seek agreement, approval or comfort.

A correct identification of "no critical breakpoint" is itself a valid outcome.

The sole purpose of this material is to expose the architectural structure of Aureum One to adversarial scrutiny, with emphasis on:

- failure modes
- governance boundaries
- evidence integrity
- responsibility limits

This document assumes the reader is capable of identifying structural weaknesses without guidance.

**This architecture is not optimized for efficiency, scale or economic performance.**  
**It is optimized for failure visibility and authority containment.**

Discomfort with the conclusions of this document is expected and not considered a design flaw.
